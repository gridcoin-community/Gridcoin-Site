Network Analytics

Daily snapshots of Gridcoin network activity since September 2020, derived from the Scraper's converged superblock statistics.

Mandatory release (labeled at top of line) Block-version activation (post-grace, labeled vN) Year boundary (Jan 1) Quarter boundary (Apr / Jul / Oct 1)

CPID activity over time

Each data point is one day. The blue line (left axis) is the count of distinct CPIDs that appeared in the superblock that day with positive magnitude. The green and red lines (right axis) are the daily gross flows: Churn in (active today, not yesterday) and Churn out (active yesterday, not today). Any dataset can be hidden by clicking its entry in the legend. Values on the very first and last day of the series — and on days adjacent to a data gap — are undefined.

MRC (Manual Research Claim) payments per day

Daily volume of MRC research-reward payments, stacked by where each GRC ends up: net to researcher, foundation fee, and staker fee. The dotted red line on the secondary axis is the per-day count of MRCs paid (typically much smaller than the GRC amounts, hence its own scale). All series are smoothed with a centered 14-day moving average (each point is the mean of the surrounding 14 days, half before and half after) to suppress the day-to-day spikes from individual large MRC claims while preserving the longer-term trend. The x-axis is aligned to the CPID- activity chart above; MRC support didn't exist before v12 (block height 2 671 700, mainnet 2022-08-28), so the leading portion of the series is zero by construction. Sourced via getmrcinfo RPC bracketed by daily UTC block boundaries.

Project whitelist churn

Count of projects present in the day's superblock (blue, left axis) and day-over-day additions / drops (green / red, right axis). Note the protocol rule: each in-superblock project gets an equal share of the network-wide 115 000 magnitude pool, so this count directly sets the per-project baseline (115 000 / N).

Active CPIDs per project over time

One line per project — each day's count of distinct CPIDs with positive magnitude on that project. Click a project name in the legend to hide or show its line. The set of projects changes over time; lines disappear when a project drops out of the superblock and reappear if it's re-added.

Top researchers by lifetime magnitude

Default ranking is by total observed magnitude (sum of daily magnitude across every day the CPID appeared in the observed window). Because the observed history begins on 2020-09-09, CPIDs that were contributing earlier are indirectly penalised — but this remains the most honest cumulative measure for the captured window. Click any numeric header to re-rank. Use the project filter to restrict the ranking to one project's contributors.
