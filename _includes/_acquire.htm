Sync With the Blockchain

  • Before your wallet can do anything, it needs to be synced (the green tick under connections means you are synced)
  • If you don't want to sync from 0, then you can download a snapshot of the blockchain from clicking File -> Snapshot Download or using the -snapshotdownload command line argument
    • The snapshot will only bring you up to 80-90% sync. Allow it to continue syncing after the snapshot has been extracted.
    • If you want to apply it directly, you can download the snapshot here. To use this, open the folder (Linux = .GridcoinResearch/, Windows = %appdata%\GridcoinResearch) and delete only the blk00x.dat files, txleveldb folder, and the accrual folder, and copy the files from the snapshot
  • If you are unable to acquire connections to the Gridcoin network, attempt the following:
    • Verify you're running the latest version of the Gridcoin client, as there are frequent updates. Updates are posted on the GitHub Release Page.
    • Verify that your computer's time is in the correct timezone.
    • Investigate whether or not your ISP, firewall, or anti-virus is blocking P2P traffic.
    • Ask for help on any community platform

Get Some Gridcoin

  • In Gridcoin's Proof-of-Stake (PoS) system, BOINC rewards are given when users stake, along with an additional 10 GRC.
  • Since Gridcoin 5.4 you don't have to stake on your own to get BOINC rewards. Instead you can get your rewards by clicking a button and paying a small fee. See this wiki entry for further details.
    • For monthly staking, a balance of 5000 GRC is recommended.
    • The rate you are rewarded for your BOINC work is the rate you mint PoS blocks because BOINC rewards are layered on top of these blocks.
    • Note: Your balance does not affect the amount of the payout, only how frequent. You also need to wait around 16 hours before any coins received are able to stake.
