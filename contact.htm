How to get in contact with us!

Our large userbase is spread across the following chat platforms:

  • Gridcoin's Telegram channel
  • Gridcoin's Slack channel
  • Gridcoin's Discord channel
  • /r/gridcoin subreddit
  • #Gridcoin Steemit tag

If you're looking to report security concerns:

  • See the official Gridcoin Security Disclosure Document.
  • Email "security@gridcoin.us" to get the team's attention.
  • Jump into the Slack channel or the Telegram channel and ask for an administrator.

If you have non-critical Gridcoin client issues/bugs you wish to raise awareness of, please submit an issue on the GitHub repo.

If you're interested in collaborating towards Gridcoin's future development, don't hesitate to join Slack/Telegram/Discord/GitHub to begin working with others on your ideas.
